One Piece Characters (Episodes 1–100)

Monkey D. Luffy

Captain of the Straw Hats with Gum-Gum Fruit powers.

Dreams of becoming King of the Pirates.

Loyal and fearless protector of his friends.

Roronoa Zoro

Three-sword swordsman aiming to be the best.

Strong and determined crew first mate.

Devoted to Luffy and his goals.

Nami

Navigator with weather and cartography skills.

Escaped Arlong’s control to join the crew.

Wants to draw the complete world map.

Usopp

Sniper and inventor with a big heart.

Dreams of becoming a brave warrior of the sea.

Grows braver with each adventure.

Sanji

Crew’s cook with powerful kicks.

Seeks the All Blue, a legendary sea.

Chivalrous and protective of women.

Red Leg Zeff

Chef and captain of the Baratie restaurant.

Sanji’s strict but caring mentor and father figure.

Lost a leg saving Sanji and taught him to fight and cook.

Patty

One of the cooks at the Baratie.

Loyal and often seen supporting Zeff and Sanji.

Participates in protecting the restaurant during crises.

Carne

Baratie cook with a calm demeanor.

Works closely with Patty and Zeff.

Helps maintain the restaurant’s daily operations.

Gin

Former crewmate of Captain Kuro.

Reformed and working as a waiter at the Baratie.

Helps defend the restaurant during Don Krieg’s attack.

Coby

Timid Marine aspiring to be an admiral.

Inspired by Luffy to grow stronger.

Represents justice and growth.

Shanks

Red Hair Pirates captain and Four Emperor.

Inspired Luffy to become a pirate.

Lost an arm protecting Luffy as a child.

Smoker

Marine captain with Smoke-Smoke Fruit powers.

Strict sense of justice and Luffy’s pursuer.

Determined to uphold Marine law.

Arlong

Fish-man pirate who ruled Nami’s hometown.

Forces Nami into servitude.

Defeated by Luffy at Arlong Park.

Kuroobi

Arlong Pirates’ fish-man and martial artist.

Fierce and loyal to Arlong.

Defeated by Sanji.

Chew

Strong Arlong Pirate fish-man.

Fights against the Straw Hats.

Loyal to Arlong’s cause.

Gaimon

Pirate trapped in a treasure chest.

Eccentric and friendly islander.

Helps the Straw Hats briefly.

Kumashi

Marine soldier under Captain Morgan.

Fights against Luffy in early episodes.

Part of corrupt Marine forces.

Masira

Marine soldier serving Captain Morgan.

Opposes Luffy in Shell Town.

Defeated early in the story.

Helmeppo

Captain Morgan’s spoiled son.

Trains under Coby to improve.

Represents growth in Marines.

Captain Morgan

Corrupt Marine captain of Shell Town.

Abused power until defeated by Luffy.

Early example of Marine corruption.

Buggy the Clown

Pirate with Chop-Chop Fruit powers.

Comedic but dangerous early antagonist.

Leads Buggy Pirates with luck and cunning.

Mohji & Richie

Buggy’s comic relief henchmen.

Often fail at their jobs.

Not serious threats to Straw Hats.

Alvida

First antagonist, a pirate captain.

Becomes stronger after a Devil Fruit.

Later serves as comic relief.

Captain Kuro

Cunning pirate plotting to kill Kaya.

Called “Kuro of a Thousand Plans.”

Defeated by Straw Hats at Syrup Village.

Gin

Kuro’s loyal right-hand man.

Skilled swordsman and tactician.

Supports Kuro’s schemes.

Don Krieg

Ruthless pirate with a heavily armed fleet.

Attacks the Baratie restaurant.

Defeated by Zoro.

Dr. Kureha

Eccentric doctor on Drum Island.

Mentors Chopper despite rough attitude.

Very old but sharp and skilled.

Hiriluk

Optimistic doctor and Chopper’s mentor.

Dreamed of curing all diseases.

His death deeply affects Chopper.

Laboon

Giant whale waiting at Reverse Mountain.

Symbolizes friendship and loyalty.

Linked to Brook’s story.

Nojiko

Nami’s adoptive sister.

Supports Nami and their village.

Shares a strong family bond.

Kaya

Kind girl from Syrup Village.

Targeted by Captain Kuro.

Saved by Usopp and crew.
